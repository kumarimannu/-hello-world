ABOUT VIDYA JYOTI SCHOOL

VIDYA JYOTI SCHOOL , situated at TATA STEEL COMPLEX COLONY, GAMHARIA is supported by TATA STEEL GROWTH SHOP since it came into existence on 5th August 1976.

Initially it was a feeder school to Tisco Schools in Jamhedpur. It is owned by Tata Growth Shop.

The school is affiliated to CBSE Board New Delhi till senior secondary level-Std-XII.The school follows the guidelines regulation of the CBSE. The school complies with all the instruction and regulations given by district education office.

The school provides instructions on the following subjects: English, Hindi, Maths , Science, Social Science, Computer (I.T) ,Sanskrit. The medium of instruction is English. It is co- educational school.

The mission of the school is to increase educational footprints in the areas of quality Secondary Education, Professional and Technical Education and Occupational Training and serve the growing aspirations of citizens of Gamharia, Adityapur and at large Jamshedpur.

Vidya Jyoti School was founded with the Vision that it will emerge as a centre of academic excellence. The school is committed to expanding the horizons of its students through use of innovative teaching methods, sharing of knowledge, skill development, team building coupled with the freedom to excel at an individual pace. The school believes that all growth must be anchored in sound values that will guide each student to become a responsible citizen of our country.

Vidya Jyoti School seeks to inculcate in its students, the ethos of excellence based on aspirations rather than remain limited by their perceived capability. The endeavour of the school is to ensure a holistic development of the student's personality by nurturing the pursuit of knowledge, cultivating and appreciation for the arts, literature, sports and games, as well as instilling high regard for human values and social consciousness.

Vidya Jyoti has adopted English as the medium of instruction and resolves to create citizens of the world, rooted in the best Indian traditions.

Our endeavour shall be to work as a team with all parents to offer our students constant and meaningful opportunities for both academic excellence as well as the assimilation of human values. The school will provide the impetus which will inspire each student to be professionally excellent, responsible and upright member of our society.
